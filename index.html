About Me

Who I am

Hi, I’m Anastasia Hinton—a passionate game developer and senior at Purdue University, majoring in Game Development and Design. I love blending art, design, and technology to create meaningful player experiences. With a strong drive to learn and collaborate, I aim to contribute to innovative projects that push the boundaries of creativity.

Skills

I’m proficient in industry tools like Photoshop, Procreate, Illustrator, DreamWeaver, Visual Studio, Unreal Engine, Godot and Blender

Skilled in programming languages such as Java, Javascript, HTML, C, C++, Lua, and Python. I’m adaptable, detail-oriented, and persistent in refining game elements through iterative design.

What Drives Me

I became a game designer to “pay it forward.” My love for games began when I first played kirby on a friend’s gameboy as a kid, and once I got my own, I was hooked. But games became more than just entertainment, they were a lifeline during some tough years, giving me a space to feel powerful when life left me feeling powerless. I want to create games that bring the same joy and emotional support I found in them to others. Innovation and meaningful work motivate me. I aim to create with integrity, knowing that great games are built not just on opportunity but on value, something I strive to bring to every project I work on.
