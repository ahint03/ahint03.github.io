About the Game

Oathbound is a collaborative studio game project focused on worldbuilding, environment design, and narrative-driven gameplay. The project was developed by a large interdisciplinary team including programmers, artists, designers, and writers. The goal of the project was to build a cohesive game world supported by strong visual direction and environmental storytelling.

My Role

On Oathbound, I worked primarily as a concept artist focusing on environment sketches, architectural concepts, and visual exploration. My work helped establish the visual tone of environments and structures used throughout the project. I created concept sketches used as reference for modeling and environment building, and explored different visual directions through iterative sketching and design variations.

Design Process

My concept process typically started with quick exploratory sketches to establish shape language and structure. From there, I refined promising ideas into more readable environment concepts and architectural designs. These concepts were used as references for modeling and environment layout within the game engine.
