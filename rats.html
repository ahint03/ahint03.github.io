Lab Rats

A third-person platformer that combines creativity and gameplay innovation.

Overview

Dive into a whimsical journey to retrieve lost stars and return them to constellations. This project features a mix of 2D cartoonish characters and stylized 3D environments, with unique mechanics like grapple hooks, jump pads, and low gravity!
