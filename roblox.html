Overview

Treasure Hunt is an adventure game where players traverse dynamic islands, searching for hidden treasures scattered across procedurally generated terrain. Players locate glowing treasures using visual and audio cues that grow stronger as they approach hidden objects. Each treasure rewards coins used to compete for leaderboard positions and improve replayability.
