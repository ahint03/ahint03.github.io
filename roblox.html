Treasure Hunt

With vibrant environments, a competitive edge, and endless replayability, Treasure Hunt is a thrilling game of exploration

Overview

Treasure Hunt is an adventure game where players traverse dynamic islands, searching for hidden treasures scattered across procedurally generated terrain.

Armed with keen observation, you must locate glowing treasures while listening for subtle sound cues that grow louder as you draw closer.

Each treasure rewards you with valuable coins to climb the leaderboards and showcase your prowess.
